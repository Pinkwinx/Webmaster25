Our Ethical Sourcing

All of PIOURE’s ingredients are sourced from local suppliers and farmers. We are committed to finding sustainable plant-based alternatives to meat and are partnered with companies like The Tofoo Co. and Biona.

Before we cultivate our weekly menu, PIOURE’s team researches recipes and visits local farmers' markets to connect with growers. This ensures the highest quality and sustainability in every ingredient.
