About Us

PIOURE incorporates aspects of traditional Southeast Asian cuisine and combines culture and innovation. Our entire menu is 100% plant based and uses sustainably sourced ingredients. At PIOURE, we offer a unique dining experience with our weekly rotating menu that allows guests to span their taste buds across a multitude of countries. Located in downtown L.A. in the heart of Chinatown, we welcome everyone to come and immerse themselves in PIOURE.
